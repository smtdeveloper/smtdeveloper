## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am **Samet AKCA**, also known as **SMTcoder**.

I am a **Full Stack Developer & Team Lead** with experience across **backend, frontend, mobile, database, DevOps, deployment, and product development**.

I started coding in **2018** and have been professionally involved in software development since **2021**. During this journey, I have contributed to **100+ projects, features, modules, and production improvements** across freelance, startup, corporate, and product-based environments.

Currently, I work at **TAMSData Software Technologies** as a **Full Stack Developer & Team Lead**, contributing to the development of **Circudata**, a platform focused on:

- Digital Product Passport
- Product traceability
- Sustainability intelligence
- Compliance workflows
- CBAM and carbon-related processes
- Supply chain data management
- QR, GTIN, DPP and product lifecycle flows

My role is not limited to writing code. I take ownership of the full software development lifecycle — from understanding the product need to designing the architecture, building the feature, reviewing code, deploying to production, and improving the system after release.

I also work as a team lead in a **20+ person software team**, where I support technical planning, task breakdown, code reviews, mentoring, and coordination between frontend, backend, product, and deployment processes.
